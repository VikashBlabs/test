Carer's Allowance Grant

The Carer's Allowance is a weekly social welfare payment to people who provide full-time care to someone because of their age, disability, or illness (including mental illness).

  • This payment recognizes the vital role of carers in our communities.
  • Eligibility depends on your means and the level of care you provide.
Learn about Carer's Allowance

What is the Fair Deal Scheme?

You can apply for financial support to help pay for the cost of care in a nursing home through the Nursing Homes Support Scheme, also known as the Fair Deal scheme.

  • The scheme is designed to make nursing home care more affordable for everyone.
  • Your contribution is based on an assessment of your income and assets.
Find out about the Fair Deal
